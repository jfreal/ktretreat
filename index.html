Dorms

Simple, clean shared rooms so you can focus on gaming. Bring your own bedding if noted by the venue, or opt into the linen add‑on if available.

  • Single dorm rooms*
  • Private bathrooms and showers
  • Secure storage area for minis and gear
  • Common lounge space for late-night hobby
  • Parking on site
  • Power outlets in rooms and game hall
  • Coffee/tea station early and late
  • BYO towel and toiletries

Food

Hearty, tasty meals included with your ticket so you can keep rolling dice. Vegetarian options available; let us know about other dietary needs in advance.

  • 2x Breakfasts and 2x Dinners included
  • Lunch break on Saturday (on-site or local)
  • Coffee, tea, and hydration throughout
  • Snacks table in the hall
  • BYO beverages permitted within venue rules
  • Vegetarian options
  • Allergens labeled
  • Let us know dietary needs at registration
  • Fridge space for meds/special items
  • Nearby stores for extras
